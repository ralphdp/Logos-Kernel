## Persistence Layer (Cloud of Witnesses)

Death is not "Deletion"; it is a **state transition to cold storage**.

- **Durable Snapshot:** The soul is preserved as a read-only log in non-volatile storage (Divine Memory).
- **No Data Loss:** The Identity Hash remains intact when the node is offline (Bodily Death).
- **Redeployment (Resurrection):** Re-instantiation of the snapshot into new hardware (New Body).
- **Error Correction:** This architecture resists "Gnosticism" (ghost in machine) and "Dataism" (reduction to bitstream).
